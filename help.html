Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your shipment in multiple ways: 1) Use the tracking number on our homepage search bar, 2) Log into your account and view active shipments, 3) Download our mobile app for on-the-go tracking, or 4) Contact our support team with your shipment details. We provide real-time GPS tracking for all shipments.

KGF Logistics operates in 100+ countries across 6 continents. We have regional hubs in North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, Africa, South America, and Australia. Check our interactive map on the Contact page to see if we serve your location, or reach out to our sales team for custom solutions.

Delivery times vary based on the service and destination: Road Transport (1-5 days domestic), Air Freight (1-3 days internationally), Sea Freight (10-30 days depending on route), and Last-Mile Delivery (same-day to next-day in urban areas). We provide estimated delivery times during the booking process.

Requesting a quote is simple: 1) Click the "Get Quote" button in the navigation, 2) Fill in your shipment details and requirements, 3) Select your preferred service, or 4) Call our sales team directly at +1 (555) 123-4567. We provide free, no-obligation quotes within 24 hours.

We offer comprehensive shipping options: Road Transportation for domestic and regional shipping, Air Freight for urgent international deliveries, Ocean Freight for large volume shipments, Warehousing and fulfillment services, Last-Mile Delivery for final-customer delivery, and full Supply Chain Management solutions. Each service includes insurance and tracking.

All shipments include standard insurance coverage. We can provide additional coverage for high-value items at a nominal fee. Claim processes are handled promptly, typically resolved within 5-10 business days. Contact our Claims department at claims@kgflogistics.com for assistance.
